Plant Paradise

Create a colorful plant or flower collage from cut-out shapes.

Details

Tools: Glue, scissors, 11x17 paper, newspaper, old magazines

Participants: 3–4 people

Time: 20–30 minutes

Instructions

  • if (you are starting) then {sketch your plant design and plan roles}
  • if (materials are needed) then {gather magazines, newspaper, scissors, glue, paper}
  • if (creating background) then {cover canvas with black & white shapes, no squares}
  • if (building plant) then {cut pastel/muted shapes from magazines, no squares}
  • if (assembling) then {glue plant onto background}
  • if (adding depth) then {use small shapes, light colors front, dark colors back}
  • if (adding title) then {cut out bold words from magazines}
  • if (finished) then {layer more pieces to increase texture}
